You've Got Questions?

We Have Answers!

Why hire remotely?

Hiring people remotely can open up a world of opportunities. At Consultancy Oasis, we recommend that all team members have a common language (English) and hold regular meetings with remote hires. We suggest using modern tools such as Slack and Jira to communicate, collaborate, and commit code. Plus, don't forget the importance of meeting face-to-face - the cost of travel is often worth it for building deeper connections!

Where is the best place to hire remotely?

The boring answer is of course: "It depends". In Consultancy Oasis we began with a focus on the Middle East and Europe, but over the years we have expanded globally. More companies should be open to going global. But if managing different time zones is not for you, then focus on your own time zone. Plus or minus one hour.

How much does it cost to hire a good developer?

As with anything else, quality costs. Giving an assessment on price depends on these 3 factors: Level of experience. The more experienced, the higher the cost. Senior developers are in demand. Technology stack. Some technologies are more in-demand than others. Unity and Unreal Engine developers are highly sought after. Geography. Some countries have lower living expenses than others.

Should I offer ownership to a developer?

Offering shares, partnership, or options have become very popular. The idea got traction in Silicon Valley. Since it has become quite standard for innovative growth companies. We support the idea, but not in all cases. In a lot of countries ownership or options are not understood. It is a more recognized value in the Western World.

Should my team member be able to speak English?

In most cases: Yes! In some countries, the English level is less good. Both countries have thousands of very skilled developers. In these cases, a project owner acts as a middleman. This person speaks and writes English. This is an extra level between you and the developer.

How can I find a developer myself?

There are a lot of ways to find developers without using a company like Consultancy Oasis. The downside is that it has become much harder for the untrained person to get any results. Good developers get offers weekly if not daily. Gaining interest is hard, and often the negotiation will be tough. If your company is a startup, and if you are on a budget, it is even harder.
